📝 Essay Writing

Learn how to write different essays.

Simple essay structures, main points, and writing tips.

📌 Basic Essay Structure

Introduction: Hook, background information, thesis statement.

Body Paragraphs: Topic sentence, evidence, explanation, connection.

Conclusion: Restate thesis, summarize main points, final thought.

🎨 Descriptive Essay

Purpose: To describe a person, place, object, or experience using details.

Main points: Sensory details, imagery, strong adjectives, clear organization.

Tip: Show the reader what something looks, sounds, feels, smells, or tastes like.

📖 Narrative Essay

Purpose: To tell a story or personal experience.

Main points: Characters, setting, conflict, events, lesson or reflection.

Tip: Write in order and make the ending show what you learned.

⚖️ Argumentative Essay

Purpose: To prove a claim using reasons and evidence
